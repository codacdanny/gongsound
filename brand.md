# Brand — Gongsound Entertainment

_Status: active_

Culturally rooted entertainment company out of **Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria**.
Record label, artist management, music production, events & tours. Afro-luxury,
regal, sound-driven. Tagline territory: _We amplify culture._

**Metallic gold gradient:** `linear-gradient(180deg,#F7D877 0%,#E6B84C 45%,#A9781C 100%)`
Used on display headlines and key numerals to evoke the foiled logo.

## Typography

- **Display** — Bricolage Grotesque (700–800). Oversized editorial headlines.
- **Serif accent** — Fraunces (italic). Poetic emphasis words ("culture", "legacy").
- **Body / UI / labels** — Inter. Labels are UPPERCASE with wide tracking.

## Voice

Proud, cultural, cinematic. Short declaratives. "We amplify culture." "The journey
begins at home." Avoid corporate filler — speak like a movement, not a company.

## Motion

Awwwards-grade: Lenis smooth/inertia scroll, GSAP ScrollTrigger reveals, parallax,
gold soundwave canvas, marquees, custom cursor. All gated behind
`prefers-reduced-motion`.
